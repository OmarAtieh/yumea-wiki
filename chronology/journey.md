## The Asset Pipeline: The Unsolved Problem

This is what blocked me in late 2025. Not perfectionism about design â€” practical complexity about production.

### What the Pipeline Needs to Do

1. **Generate** â€” AI tools (Midjourney, Leonardo, Nano Banana) create art from prompts
2. **Validate** â€” Check consistency with style guide, readability at card size, color accessibility
3. **Integrate** â€” Add to wiki, export for print-and-play, version control

### Where I Got Stuck

- Prompt engineering for 70+ unique cards (nightmares, personas, blueprints)
- Ensuring visual consistency across AI generations
- Validating that art works at actual card size
- Workflow: generate â†’ review â†’ iterate â†’ approve â†’ integrate

### The Realization

I can't be prompt engineer, art director, and game designer simultaneously. Not with a day job and three kids.

I need:
- **Prompt templates** that produce consistent results
- **Validation criteria** (checklist, not subjective "does this feel right?")
- **Batch workflow** (generate 10, review 10, approve/reject, move on)
- **Integration automation** (symlink to wiki, auto-update)

### February 2026: Resuming With Support

Today's work: documenting the pipeline requirements, identifying what can be delegated, what needs my creative eye, and what can be automated.
