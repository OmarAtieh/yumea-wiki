## Agent Roles

### 1. Design Validator
**Purpose**: Critique mechanics, suggest simplifications, identify fun-killers

**Questions for each system**:
1. What problem does it solve?
2. Does it create more complexity than value?
3. Would an 8-year-old understand it?
4. Does it serve the emotional journey?
5. Can it be simplified or eliminated?

### 2. Market Scout
**Purpose**: Analyze market fit, suggest publisher positioning

**Research areas**:
- 3-5 comparable successful games
- What makes Yumea unique
- Publisher fit analysis
- Kickstarter feasibility
- Market risks

### 3. Production Manager
**Purpose**: Timelines, milestones, shipping checklist

**Deliverables**:
- Production milestone checklist
- Cost estimates (500/1000/2000 units)
- Timeline estimate
- Risk factors
- Recommended production path

### 4. Playtest Protocol
**Purpose**: Design playtest procedures, feedback frameworks

**Protocols needed**:
1. First playtest (Carlie, private, low-stakes)
2. Blind playtest (strangers, no Omar present)
3. Feedback forms for different player types
4. Success metrics
5. Iteration decision framework

### 5. Art Pipeline Advisor
**Purpose**: AI art workflows, cost estimates, style guides

**Focus**:
- AI-assisted pipeline (Midjourney, DALL-E, etc.)
- Style consistency across 50+ cards
- Printing requirements (300dpi, bleed)
- Cost comparison: AI vs. illustrator
- Component art requirements
