## Turn Structure

Each turn has 3 phases, in order:

### Phase 1: Place a Tile

Draw a terrain tile from the bag. Place it adjacent to existing tiles.

**Why placement matters:**
- Matching terrain types create harmony chains
- Adjacent tiles activate persona abilities
- Position affects anchor completion

**Example:** You place a Water tile next to an existing Water tile. This creates a harmony chain â€” mark 1 ğŸ•Šï¸ on the harmony track.

### Phase 2: Check Tension

If your tile placement created harmony â†’ nothing happens.

If no harmony created â†’ increase Tension by 1.

If Tension crosses a threshold (5, 10, 15) â†’ **Draw a Nightmare.**

**Nightmare cards:**
- **Whispers (1-5 Tension):** Minor effects, easily managed
- **Echoes (6-10 Tension):** Moderate threats, require response
- **Reverberations (11+ Tension):** Serious dangers, can corrupt

Draw from the appropriate tier. Read it aloud. Place it on the board (it covers terrain, blocking harmony there).

## Anchors: How to Win

Anchor cards show pattern objectives:
- "3 Water tiles in a triangle"
- "5 tiles total, at least 2 Courage symbols"
- "Path connecting 2 specific tiles"

**To complete an Anchor:**
1. Pattern must exist on the board
2. Spend 2 AP and required harmony (shown on card)
3. Collect the Anchor card (worth victory points)

**First Anchor completed:** Draw new Anchor, increase all persona energy by 1.

**Third Anchor completed:** **You win.**

---

## Nightmares: How You Lose

Nightmares sit on tiles, blocking harmony there. They have effects:

- **Fear** â€” Players adjacent lose 1 energy
- **Despair** â€” Cannot play cards with Hope symbols
- **Isolation** â€” Adjacent tiles don't count as adjacent
- **Chaos** â€” Random tile flips to opposite side

**Removing Nightmares:**
- Purification cards (matching the Nightmare's emotion)
- Certain persona abilities
- Completing Anchors (removes all Nightmares from completed pattern)

**Corruption:** If a Nightmare stays on the board 3 full rounds, it corrupts. Corrupted tiles are permanently damaged â€” no harmony, no abilities work there.

## Winning & Losing

**Victory:**
- Complete 3 Anchors

**Defeat (any of these):**
- Tension reaches 20

- 5+ tiles corrupted
- Decks run out (rare, but possible)

## Family Mode (Simpler)

For younger players or first games:
- Remove Reverberations (tier 3 nightmares)
- Start with 4 energy instead of 2
- Corruption takes 5 rounds instead of 3
- Nightmares have no ongoing effects (just block tiles)

Keep the core experience, reduce complexity.
