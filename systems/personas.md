# Personas

> *The 12 Dreamscapers ‚Äî emotional archetypes who guide the dreamer through their subconscious journey.*

---

## Overview

Yumea features **12 unique personas**, each representing a distinct emotional archetype and playstyle. Each persona has:

- **Core Ability**: Always-active passive or triggered effect
- **Ultimate Ability**: Powerful once-per-game effect
- **Emotional Affinity**: Primary emotional resonance
- **Playstyle**: Strategic approach to the dreamscape

## Design Philosophy

Each persona embodies an emotional approach to healing:
- **Scholar/Architect**: Methodical, structured
- **Dreamweaver/Child**: Intuitive, hopeful  
- **Brave/Pathfinder**: Direct, courageous
- **Visionary/Artisan**: Perceptive, creative
- **Trickster**: Unpredictable, adaptive
- **Conduit/Envoy**: Collaborative, supportive
- **Bloom**: Growth-oriented, momentum-driven
