# Personas

> *The 12 Dreamscapers ‚Äî emotional archetypes who guide the dreamer through their subconscious journey.*

---

## Overview

Yumea features **12 unique personas**, each representing a distinct emotional archetype and playstyle. Personas provide:

- **Core Ability**: Always-active passive or triggered effect
- **Ultimate Ability**: Powerful once-per-game (or conditionally reusable) effect
- **Affinity Bonus**: Optional advanced rule for emotional alignment bonuses
- **Awakening**: Campaign/deluxe mode progression unlock

## Persona Selection Variants

### Standard Draft
1. Deal 2 random personas to each player
2. Choose 1, pass other to left
3. Final selection from 2 options

### Emotional Arc Draft
- Players choose based on desired emotional journey
- Goal cards revealed before persona selection
- Thematic: "I want to play through Fear this game" ‚Üí Choose Courage-aligned persona

### Tutorial Pool
- Restricted to: Architect, Scholar, Bloom, Pathfinder
- Simpler abilities, more visible effects
- Recommended for first game
